Simple to Learn

Four Steps to Mastery

The rules take a minute to learn. The strategy takes a lifetime to master.

01

Draw Your Hand

Start with 4 cards, each with a unique combination of color, shape, and number. 64 unique cards plus 2 wilds make every game different.

02

Match Patterns

Place cards on the board so every line follows one rule: each attribute (color, shape, number) must be either all the same or all different.

03

Score & Strategize

Every card in your lines adds to your score. Plan multi-card combos, extend existing lines, and aim for the highest-scoring plays.

04

Win the Game

When the deck runs out and no more moves remain, the player with the highest score wins. Challenge harder AI or invite friends to compete.
